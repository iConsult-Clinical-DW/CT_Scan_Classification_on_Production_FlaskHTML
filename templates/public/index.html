CT Scan Image Classification

Brief Overview:

This Data Science product is aimed at aiding healthcare industries accurately make patient level disease predictions by utilizing the trained Image Classification model (time series forecast model) and providing the patient's CT scan image as an input. A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model has been trained to achieve this.
